About Us

Agromech International is a proud Sri Lankan enterprise dedicated to transforming the agricultural sector through innovation, quality, and community empowerment. With a strong foundation in manufacturing and selling agricultural machinery, our mission extends beyond technology — we cultivate opportunities.

We partner with local farmers by purchasing their produce and helping them scale operations through access to reliable tools and machinery. Our cultivation efforts ensure food security and quality, while our food processing and export initiatives contribute to foreign exchange earnings for the country.

In addition to our local impact, Agromech International has formed a strategic affiliation with Rayans LLC in New Zealand, strengthening our global footprint and commitment to international quality standards.

Vision

To lead Sri Lanka’s agricultural transformation by empowering farmers, innovating in machinery, and expanding the global reach of our nation’s harvest.

Mission

At Agromech International, we strive to empower local farmers and small-scale entrepreneurs. Our mission is to cultivate high-quality agricultural products while providing top-notch agricultural machinery to enhance productivity. We are committed to adding value through food processing and exports, enriching the lives of our people and boosting our nation’s economy.

Our Services

  • Manufacturing agricultural machinery
  • Selling agricultural machinery
  • Exporting agricultural machinery
  • Cultivating agri products
  • Purchasing crops from local farmers
  • Processing food products
  • Exporting processed food items
